Have You Ever...

We've all been there before - stuck on hold in a seemingly endless queue with that dreaded 800 number on the other line. You have more things on your priority list higher than sitting on hold for 20 minutes, and not even really being sure you'll talk to a person once the wait is over. The thing is, you really need some information about what that extended warranty covers, or what have you. That's where handy tools like Olark Chatbot comes in to play! Chatbot is a live chat tool that can be integrated with any website, and allows for direct, live interaction with customers. This poses less risk to a customer than calling an 800 number, and less risk means better customer relations, which means a more successful business!
